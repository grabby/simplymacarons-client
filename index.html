Our Story

Simply Macarons began as a passion project in our home kitchen in Victoria, BC. What started with a simple desire to create the perfect French macaron has grown into a beloved local business.

We use only the finest ingredients - fresh eggs, pure almond flour, and real fruit extracts. Each macaron is handcrafted using traditional French techniques, ensuring that delicate texture and perfect flavor balance that makes these treats so special.

Whether you're celebrating a special occasion, hosting an event, or simply treating yourself, our macarons bring a touch of Parisian elegance to every moment.
